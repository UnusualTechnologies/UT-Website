**Location:** Fully Remote - UK based (flexible hours but expected to have availability during core office hours)

**Position:** Fractional/Part-Time (this can be on an employment or self employed contract basis)

**Compensation:** £30,000 Base Stipend + High-Upside Active Revenue Share (Up to 30% post-break-even)

**Experience:** We are looking for someone who has strong professional experience working at this level from concept to launch, being able to work autonomously and proactively. 

### **Our Studio & Our New Chapter**

For years, we have been elite technical creators. We use game engines to build highly polished interactive experiences, gamification systems, and immersive tech for museums and corporate clients. We know exactly how to build incredible software.

Now, we are taking an exciting leap into commercial video games. Our challenge is ensuring we build exactly what players want so we want to give a say in the design to our marketing executive who will own this market validation process, and then build the audience as we build the game.

### **This Role**

While this is a leadership role in terms of strategy, we are a small, flat team. You won’t be managing a large department or an agency; this is an exciting opportunity to be a self-starting solo operator who is comfortable researching data, testing hooks, creating content, and building a community with your own hands.

### **Day to day duties**

- **Identify Market Potential:** Use data platforms (SteamDB, VG Insights, GameDiscoverCo) to analyse Steam genres, identify under-served market niches, and help our dev team decide what to build next.
- **Validate Ideas Early (Smoke Testing):** Help us create concept storefronts, target hooks, and short alpha-trailers to test player interest and drive early wishlists before full production begins.
- **Own the Steam Funnel:** Completely manage our store presence, from tag optimization and capsule art A/B testing to navigating Steam Next Fests.
- **Organic Content Creation:** Cut gameplay clips and craft engaging, authentic short-form videos (TikTok, YouTube Shorts) that highlight our game mechanics.
- **Community & Creator Outreach:** Establish our Discord/Reddit ecosystems and directly pitch our prototypes to the specific influencers and streamers who love our target genres.

### **This Is a Perfect Fit For You If…**

- You have a passion for games and professional experience in the industry
- You view marketing as an analytical science and a psychological puzzle, not just “social media promotion.”
- You are comfortable with ambiguity and get excited by the challenge of defining a product’s market fit from scratch.
- You are highly organized, deeply reliable, and thrive when left entirely alone to manage your own schedule and priorities.
- Confidently draw in all areas of game development as part of the marketing and brand process and know when to leverage external relationships (business development, partnerships, licencing etc) to strengthen chances of commercial success

### **This Is Not For You If…**

- You prefer high-level theoretical consulting over rolling up your sleeves to write copy, edit videos, and chat with community members.
